Senior Researcher @ Tencent ARC Lab.

I am currently a senior researcher at Tencent ARC Lab, working on 3D Generative AI. I obtained my Ph.D degree at ShanghaiTech University in 2024, advised by Prof. Shenghua Gao. Before that, I received my Bachelorâ€™s degree in Computer Science from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2019.
